Inner City Open Results
Feb 14, 2009

          Judo Saskatchewan sent a team of 19 athletes to Winnipeg, MB for the Inner City Open, on February 14, 2009. The team performed well at this developmental competition with many of the athletes having outstanding performances against strong competition.

          Emily Schaan of Watrous lead the team winning the U17 63kg division beating former Juvenile national champion Manon Soulard of Manitoba 2 times in the competition and she also won the most outstanding cadet/junior athlete of the competition. Judo Saskatchewan had strong performances from Alex Delorme, Jesse Caron, Galen Morrison, Kenadee Thompson and Rebecca Hanson who all won gold medals in their respective weight classes.
