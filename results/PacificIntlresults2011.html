Pacific Senior International Results
February 19, 2011

      Saskatchewan was represented by 2 athletes at the Pacific Senior Internationals in Steveston, B.C.

      Here are the individual results:

        • Kendal McArthur from Watrous Ju-No-Ri finished 2nd in the -73Kg division
        • Chellys Vallejo from Prince Albert Judo Club finished 2nd in the -78Kg division


      Interesting Note: On Friday Dean McGarry and the 5 athletes from Lloydminster showed up at the airport and were told their flight was cancelled. They told him that they could fly them into Vancouver for Sunday morning.....the plane that they were suppose to take was hit by lightening coming from Hawaii and was taken out of commission for repairs. (Dean and the team from Lloydminster did not attend the competition)
