Sask Judo Team Gold and Bronze Medals at CWG on Day 1
The Saskatchewan Canada Winter Games Judo team is coming home with a gold and a bronze medal after competition Tuesday.

According to the Team Manager T.V. Taylor who telephoned from the site, Jordan Poliakiwski has secured a gold medal for the Saskatchewan team at the Canada Winter Games, and Kendal McArthur has secured a bronze medal .

Michael Leuschen won two and lost two and finished in Fifth place in the Over 60 kg up to and including 66 kg weight category. Matthew Furneaux won two and lost two finishing in 7th place in the Over 66 kg up to and including 73 kg weight category.


2007 Canada Winter Games Day Two - Silver and Bronze Medals.
Day two results are finished, Bethany Stever add's a Silver Medal to Team Saskatchewan and Dylan Drew Weber is coming home with a Bronze Medal .
