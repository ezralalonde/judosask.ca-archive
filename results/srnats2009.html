2009 Senior National Judo Championships
Sherbroooke, Quebec, May 22-24, 2009

      Judo Saskatchewan had a very good competition winning 1 Gold Medal, 1 Silver Medal and 2 Bronze finishing fourth in the over all medal count. Emin Sheykhislyamov from Prince Albert had an outstanding competition winning the senior national title in 90kg weight division. Emin won all four of his matches by ippon the equivalence to a knockout in boxing and in the process he defeated Alexandre Emond from Quebec in the final who is the defending Pan-Am Champion.
