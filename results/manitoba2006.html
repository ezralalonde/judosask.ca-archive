U of M Open Judo Tournament Results
December 2, 2006

A team of 31 athletes, coaches, and officials from Judo Saskatachewan traveled to the University of Manitoba Judo Tournament in Winnipeg, MB.

Of the 24 Judo Saskatchewan athletes who competed on December 2 2006, 18 came home with medals.
